About Me


Who am I?

Well, My name is Hannah and I used to be a teacher. I taught pretty much my entire time in college and a few years after I left university as well. I even went to school for biology and Neuroscience with the thought of becoming a teacher. I came to the conclusion that working with kids is a passion of mine, but being a teacher was very unfufilling.
I had also begun learning C# and video game development after highschool, and mainly did this as a hobby or creative outlet. If you could divide my brain into traits I hold, I would say that creativity would cover about 90% of it

Why Did I choose Software Development?

This is a bit convoluted so please fasten your seatbelts as you follow me down the rabbit hole.
But I have loved video games since I was a child and always wanted to create my own playable stories. I began writing plots as a 6 year old and still have them to this day. I actually turned a few of those Ideas into published comics as a teenager, but ultimately didnt being coding until 2019. I started off slow with just Unity and C# yet I found myself becoming obsessed with it. I was in university and working full time so I didnt really have time to delve deep like I wanted to.
I didnt think I was very good either. I pretty much sucked at technology if I am being honest with myself. Then the pandemic hit and gave me pretty much unlimited time in front of my computer screen as I was stuck in the house.
It was during this time that I became more confident in my skills and even branched out to different development languages. I ended up leaving school year three due to multiple factors but it gave me the perfect oppertunity to pursure my goals in software development. I ended up joining a local coding bootcamp and fell in love with the creative processes of web development.

What Are your favorite coding Languages?

C# will always have a special place in my heart. It was the first language I ever learned and continues to be my strongest, alongside JavaScript and Python.

What Has Been your Favorite Project?

I loved creating the Static StoreFront Landing Page.
It was fun to create and more so cathartic that stressful as an assignment. I love pink and think that making a webpage with all pink was something I dont really see often. Since it was a perosnal project I could be extremely experimental and try unconventioal artstyles.

Whats with all of the plants?

I adore plants!! I have dozens of them at home and continue to grow that number!! Seeing them makes me so happy and calms my nerves, so I decided to add some to my portfolio in the hopes that they can make other people smile that they do, me.
